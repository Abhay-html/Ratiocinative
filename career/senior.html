Senior Front-End Developer

Mohali, Punjab    |    Full Time

Apply Now

We are looking for a Senior Front-End Developer to join our Engineering team. This position will play an important role in driving growth and user engagement through delightful user experiences.

A Senior Front-End Developer is responsible for implementing visual and interactive elements that users engage with through their web browser when using a web application. A front-end web developer is responsible for implementing visual elements that users see and interact with in a web application.

Job Responsibilities:

  • Contribute to the overall architecture design and specification of next generation Social interface of our CRM platform.
  • Focus on scalability and building robust scalable systems using the cutting edge developments in Social Networks.
  • Should know the architecture of WordPress.
  • Taking ownership and initiatives in building innovative products providing seamless experience to the end user.
  • Strong Coding skills with Understanding of Object Oriented Programming and MVC design patterns frameworks are a must.
  • In-depth knowledge of HTML, HTML5, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, Wordpress.
  • Expertize in Twitter Bootstrap, Naming Conventions, Coding Standards, SASS and LESS.
  • Energetic, driven, self-starter with high levels of initiative.

Education & Experience:

  • 3-5 years of experience front end, Mobile Apps, consumer, commercial, and enterprise web applications.
  • The developer should have Outstanding communication skills with the ability to explain ideas and concepts to the client.
